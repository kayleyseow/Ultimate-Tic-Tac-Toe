Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a game of strategy.
"Ultimate" or "Strategic" tic-tac-toe is played on a large tic-tac -toe board,
with a small tic-tac-toe in each grid, looking somewhat similar to a sudoku board without the numbers.
The large 3x3 board is referred to as the global board
and each small 3x3 board is referred to as a local board.
